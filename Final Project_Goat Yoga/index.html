About Goat Yoga

Goat yoga is a revolutionary wellness practice that combines traditional yoga with the playful interaction of adorable goats. Originating as a novel concept, goat yoga has quickly become a popular form of animal-assisted therapy and exercise.

Benefits of Goat Yoga

  • Stress Reduction: Interacting with animals can lower cortisol levels and reduce anxiety.
  • Improved Mood: Goats' playful nature brings joy and laughter to yoga practice.
  • Unique Exercise Experience: Combines physical fitness with animal therapy.

Safety and Sanitation

We prioritize both human and animal well-being. Our goats are:

  • Regularly veterinarian-checked
  • Kept clean and groomed
  • Trained to interact safely with humans

Participants are provided with sanitizing stations and mats are cleaned thoroughly between sessions.
